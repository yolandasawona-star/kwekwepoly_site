Our History

Kwekwe Polytechnic was established to provide quality technical and vocational training to students across Zimbabwe. Over the years, it has grown into a center of excellence recognized for producing skilled graduates.

Through strong ties with industry and the community, the institution continues to support innovation and economic growth.

Our Mission

To provide quality technical, vocational, and professional education that equips learners with practical skills for employment and entrepreneurship.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in technical education, innovation, and research in Zimbabwe and beyond.
